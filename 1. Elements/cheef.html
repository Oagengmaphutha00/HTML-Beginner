How to make Delicious Mince!
  • 500g lean beef mince
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated (optional)
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Â½ tsp salt

  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. SautÃ© the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the beef mince and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Stir in the grated carrot (if using) and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Mix in the tomato paste, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour in the beef stock and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve over rice, mashed potatoes, or use it as a filling for tacos or pasta.
